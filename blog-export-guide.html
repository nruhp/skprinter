Export Packaging Requirements: Complete Compliance Checklist

Posted on Dec 20, 2025 | Category: Export

Shipping internationally? A stuck shipment at customs due to packaging non-compliance can cost lakhs. Follow this checklist.

1. ISPM-15 Compliance

If you use wooden pallets or crates, they MUST be heat-treated and stamped with ISPM-15. Corrugated pallets are exempt from this, making them a hassle-free alternative for export.

2. Moisture Resistance

Sea freight containers face high humidity ("container rain"). Standard boxes will turn into mush. For export, we use moisture-resistant kraft liners and sometimes a thin PE coating to ensure box integrity over weeks at sea.
