Our Quality Assurance Process

At SK Printers, quality isn't just a checkpoint; it's embedded in every step of our manufacturing.

1. Raw Material Testing

Every paper reel is tested for GSM, moisture content, and bursting strength before entering the production line.

2. In-Process Inspection

Automated sensors monitor corrugation flute height and glue application continuously during production.

3. Structural Testing

Finished boxes undergo Edge Crush Test (ECT) and Box Compression Test (BCT) to ensure load-bearing capacity.

4. Print Quality Check

We check for color consistency (Delta E), registration accuracy, and barcode readability.
