Shipping & Delivery Policy

1. Delivery Areas

We deliver to all 28 states and 8 union territories in India. For remote locations, please contact our support team to confirm delivery feasibility.

2. Delivery Timelines

  • Standard Items: Dispatched within 24-48 hours. Delivery in 3-5 business days.
  • Custom Orders: Manufacturing time 3-5 days + Delivery time 3-5 days.
  • Rush Orders: Expedited manufacturing (24 hrs) and Express Shipping (1-2 days) available at extra cost.

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on Volumetric Weight and destination. Free shipping is available for orders above ₹50,000 for specific zones.

4. Damages during Transit

We use heavy-duty corner protectors and shrink wrap to minimize damage. However, if you receive damaged goods, please report it within 24 hours of delivery with photos for a free replacement.

5. International Shipping

For export orders, shipping terms are governed by Incoterms 2020 (EXW, FOB, CIF as agreed). We handle all export documentation including ISPM-15 certification.
