Building the Foundation of Trust

Since 2018, SK Printers has been redefining the packaging industry with sustainability, innovation, and reliability.

Our Story

Started as a small printing press in Vellore, SK Printers has evolved into one of South India's largest cardboard manufacturing facilities. We recognized the need for high-quality, sustainable packaging for the booming e-commerce sector and pivoted our operations to meet this demand.

Today, we produce over 50,000 boxes daily and serve 500+ enterprise clients across 28 states.

Our Mission

"To provide sustainable, durable, and cost-effective packaging solutions that empower businesses to deliver their products safely anywhere in the world."
